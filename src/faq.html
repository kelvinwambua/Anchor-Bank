FAQ

How do I open a new account with Anchor Bank?

You can open a new account with Anchor Bank by visiting our nearest branch or applying online through our website.

What types of accounts does Anchor Bank offer?

We offer a range of accounts including savings accounts, checking accounts, money market accounts, and various investment options.

What are the requirements for opening a business account?

To open a business account, you'll typically need to provide business documentation such as your business license, tax identification number, and legal entity information.

Can I access my account online?

Yes, you can access your account online through our secure online banking portal or mobile app, allowing you to check balances, transfer funds, and manage transactions.

How do I report a lost or stolen debit/credit card?

If your debit or credit card is lost or stolen, please contact our customer service immediately at [phone number] to report it and request a replacement card.

What fees are associated with Anchor Bank accounts?

Our fees vary depending on the type of account and services you use. You can find detailed information about our fees in our account disclosures or by contacting customer service.

Can I set up automatic bill payments with Anchor Bank?

Yes, you can set up automatic bill payments through our online banking platform, allowing you to schedule recurring payments for your bills.

Is my money safe with Anchor Bank?

Yes, your deposits are insured by the Federal Deposit Insurance Corporation (FDIC) up to the maximum allowable limit, providing security and peace of mind.

What should I do if I suspect fraudulent activity on my account?

If you notice any suspicious activity on your account, please contact our fraud department immediately at [phone number] to report it and protect your account.

How do I apply for a loan or mortgage with Anchor Bank?

You can apply for a loan or mortgage by contacting our loan department or filling out an application online. Our team will guide you through the process and assist with your financing needs.
